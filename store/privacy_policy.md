# Privacy Policy — Smart Wallet

**Last updated: 27 June 2026**

Smart Wallet ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal income and expense ledger
developed by Stibin Augustine. This policy explains what data the app handles and
how. **Smart Wallet is built to be offline-first: your financial data lives on
your device, not on our servers.** We do not operate any backend server, we do not
have user accounts, and we do not sell your data.

## Summary

- All your transactions, budgets, savings goals, bills, and reports are stored
  **locally on your device** in an on-device database.
- We collect **no analytics**, show **no ads**, and run **no tracking**.
- Data only leaves your device in two cases, both under your control:
  1. **AI insights (optional)** — only if you add your own AI API key, a text
     summary of your finances is sent to the AI provider you chose to generate
     insights.
  2. **Export/backup (optional)** — when you tap export/share, you choose where
     the CSV/PDF file goes (e.g. Google Drive, email).

## Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally and never transmits it to us:

- Income and expense transactions, categories, and budget limits
- Savings goals and bills/subscriptions
- Receipt details you scan or enter
- App settings, including your selected currency and notification preferences

You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling
the app.

## Permissions and why they are used

- **Camera / Photos** — to scan or attach receipts. Receipt text is recognised
  **on your device** using Google ML Kit; receipt images and text are not sent to
  us.
- **Microphone** — to let you add entries by voice. Speech is processed by your
  device's on-device speech recognition.
- **Notifications and battery-optimization exemption** — to deliver reminders,
  budget alerts, and the daily insight on schedule, including when the app is
  closed. These notifications are generated locally.
- **Internet** — used only to contact the AI provider you configure (see below).

## AI insights (third-party processing — optional)

The AI features are **off by default**. They only work after you enter your own
API key for one of the supported providers (OpenRouter, Anthropic, or OpenAI).
When enabled, the app sends a **text summary of your financial data** (such as
category totals and spending trends) to that provider over an encrypted (HTTPS)
connection so it can return insights. We never receive this data.

Your use of a provider is also governed by that provider's privacy policy:

- OpenRouter — https://openrouter.ai/privacy
- Anthropic — https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
- OpenAI — https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

If you do not configure an AI key, no financial data leaves your device.

## Data sharing and selling

We do **not** sell your data and we do **not** share it with third parties for
advertising. The only outbound data flow is the optional AI processing described
above, and files you explicitly choose to export or share.

## Children's privacy

Smart Wallet is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
data from them.
